Mabuhay! Welcome to Chronicles Beyond Time, where Bohol’s cultural heritage comes to life. Chronicles Beyond Time is a blog dedicated to Bohol’s rich heritage, created to share the culture and traditions of the Boholanos with everyone. Beyond sharing, our mission is to spread awareness about the importance of protecting and preserving our cultural identity, which faces the threat of fading due to modernization, neglect, and time. As students, we believe that by showcasing Bohol’s unique stories, landmarks, and traditions through blogging, we can inspire a collective effort to safeguard these treasures for future generations, ensuring that the spirit of Bohol lives on.
