Maayong Adlaw sa inyong tanan! Welcome to Chronicles Beyond Time, an advocacy blog brought to you by STEM DOS Valkyries. We’re a passionate group of students dedicated in celebrating and preserving Bohol’s rich cultural heritage. As advocates for history and tradition, we aim to create awareness about the treasures that define our identity from timeless architecture and breathtaking landscapes to vibrant festivals and folklore passed down through generations.


This website's goal is to provide a dynamic arena for preserving and sharing the world's cultural legacy. At its core, it aims to remind us of the profound stories, beliefs, and traditions that have lasted throughout history, each expressing a distinct perspective and way of living. By exploring into the complexities of traditions, dialects, art, and history, tourists get insight into what makes each culture unique.


The site combines a variety of multimedia elements, including galleries and interactive features, to create a rich, immersive experience. Each piece of content is designed to pique users' interest and improve appreciation for cultural variety, allowing them to connect with the past and with one another. We aim to use this website to act as a global cultural exchange hub, assisting people from all walks of life in understanding, celebrating, and carrying on the legacies that create our shared human experience.
